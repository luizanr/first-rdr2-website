Arthur Morgan is the protagonist of Red Dead Redemption 2, a veteran of the Van der Linde gang led by Dutch Van der Linde. Known for his loyalty and skills in the Wild West, Arthur is a skilled criminal, but his complex morality puts him in constant internal conflict.

⋯ History and Background ⋯

Character's origin

Arthur Morgan was born in 1863 to Beatrice and Lyle Morgan. His mother died when he was young, and his father was a petty criminal who taught Arthur the ways of the outlaw. Arthur was orphaned at a young age and eventually joined the Van der Linde gang after being taken in by Dutch Van der Linde and Hosea Matthews, who became his surrogate fathers.

Key events in the character's life

    Joining the Van der Linde Gang:
  • • Arthur was taken in by Dutch Van der Linde and Hosea Matthews, who mentored him and shaped him into a formidable outlaw.
    • Relationship with Mary Linton:
  • • Arthur had a romantic relationship with Mary Linton, but their differing paths led to their separation.
    • The Blackwater Heist:
  • • A failed heist in Blackwater marks the beginning of the game’s main storyline, setting the gang on the run.
    • Diagnosis with Tuberculosis:
  • • Arthur contracts tuberculosis from a debtor named Thomas Downes, which profoundly affects his health and outlook on life.
    • Redemption:
  • • Towards the end of his life, Arthur seeks redemption for his past misdeeds, helping John Marston and his family escape the gang's clutches.

Connections with Other Characters

    Dutch van der Linde:
  • • Mentor and father figure to Arthur, though their relationship becomes strained as Dutch's behavior becomes more erratic.
    • John Marston:
  • • Brotherly figure to Arthur, with whom he shares a deep bond, despite occasional conflicts. Arthur acts as a protector and mentor to John.
    • Sadie Adler:
  • • Loyal ally who respects Arthur's strength and moral struggle. Their relationship evolves into a deep friendship based on mutual respect and shared experiences.

⋯ Appearance ⋯

Detailed physical description

Arthur is tall and muscular, with a rugged and weathered appearance due to years of living as an outlaw. He has blue eyes and brown hair, which he keeps either short or slicked back. His face is often covered in stubble or a full beard, depending on the player's choices.

Outfits and Clothing Variations

Arthur typically wears traditional cowboy attire, including a hat, coat, and boots. His outfits vary throughout the game, ranging from rugged workwear to more formal attire for specific missions. The player can customize Arthur's clothing to suit different weather conditions and personal preferences.

Visual Evolution in the Game

Arthur's appearance changes with the progression of the story, reflecting his physical and emotional state. As his illness progresses, he appears more gaunt and pale, mirroring his deteriorating health.

⋯ Personality ⋯

Role in the Gang

Senior member and enforcer, trusted by Dutch to handle critical tasks and lead the gang in various missions. Arthur's role involves planning and executing heists, protecting gang members, and managing conflicts.

Personality Traits

Arthur is loyal, introspective, and pragmatic, often conflicted about his actions and the direction of the gang. He is deeply committed to those he cares about but becomes increasingly disillusioned with Dutch's leadership. Despite his outlaw status, Arthur displays a strong moral compass, showing compassion and empathy towards those in need.

Relationships and Interactions

    Close Bonds:

  • • Dutch van der Linde, John Marston, Sadie Adler. Arthur's relationships with these characters are central to his development and the game's narrative.

    • Antagonistic:

  • • Micah Bell. Arthur's distrust and eventual animosity towards Micah highlight the internal conflicts within the gang.

⋯ Skills and Abilities ⋯

Special abilities

Arthur is an expert marksman, skilled horseman, and proficient hunter. He is also a talented artist, often sketching in his journal. His skills in combat and survival make him a versatile and valuable member of the gang.

Preferred equipment andweapons

Revolvers, rifles, lasso. Arthur's choice of weapons reflects his practical approach to combat and his proficiency in various fighting styles.

How skills are used

Arthur's skills are integral in combat, hunting, and leading various missions. His abilities make him a versatile and crucial member of the gang, capable of handling a wide range of challenges.

⋯ Importance in the Game ⋯

Character's role in the main storyline

Arthur is central to the plot, driving the gang's actions and his personal redemption arc. His journey shapes the game's narrative and influences the fate of other characters. The player's choices as Arthur can lead to different outcomes, affecting the storyline and the fates of other gang members.

Associated missions or quests

Many primary and side missions revolve around Arthur, including significant heists, protection missions, and personal quests. These missions highlight his leadership role and the various challenges he faces.

Impact on the storyline development

Arthur's actions and decisions significantly shape the game's narrative and outcomes, affecting the lives of his fellow gang members and the direction of the story. His struggle for redemption and his evolving relationships add depth to the game's storyline.

⋯ Trivia and Easter Eggs ⋯

Interesting fact

Arthur is a talented artist, frequently sketching animals and landscapes in his journal. His drawings often provide insights into his thoughts and feelings, offering a glimpse into his internal struggles.

Easter eggs in the game

Various hidden drawings and references can be found in Arthur's journal and interactions, reflecting his journey and relationships. These easter eggs often connect to other characters and events in the game, adding layers of depth and interconnectivity to the narrative.
