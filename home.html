America, 1899. The end of the Old West era began. After a robbery that goes terribly wrong in the western town of Blackwater, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee. With federal agents and the nation’s best bounty hunters on their trail, the gang must steal, plunder, and fight for survival through the harsh heart of America.. As internal divisions deepen threatening to tear the gang apart, Arthur finds himself faced with a difficult choice: to follow his own ideals or maintain loyalty to the gang that created him..
