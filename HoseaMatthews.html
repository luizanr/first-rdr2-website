Hosea Matthews is a senior member of the Van der Linde gang in Red Dead Redemption 2. As Dutch's oldest and most trusted friend, Hosea is a wise and cunning strategist who brings a sense of stability and moral guidance to the gang.

⋯ History and Background ⋯

Character's origin

Hosea Matthews was born into a poor family and turned to a life of crime at a young age. His early years were spent as a con artist, honing his skills in deception and strategy.

Key events in the character's life

    Joining the Gang:
  • • Hosea met Dutch van der Linde in his youth, and the two quickly formed a partnership based on mutual respect and shared ideals. Together, they built the Van der Linde gang.
    • Key Missions:
  • • Hosea plays a crucial role in planning and executing many of the gang's heists and schemes, including the Saint Denis bank robbery and the Valentine stagecoach heist.
    • Internal Conflicts:
  • • Hosea often acts as the voice of reason within the gang, challenging Dutch's increasingly erratic decisions and advocating for a more cautious and strategic approach.
    • Final Stand:
  • • Hosea's wisdom and moral compass make him a central figure in the gang's dynamics. His eventual death during a failed heist is a significant turning point in the game's narrative.

Connections with Other Characters

    Arthur Morgan:
  • • Hosea acts as a mentor to Arthur, offering guidance and support. Their relationship is built on trust and respect.
    • John Marston:
  • • Hosea also supports and advises John, helping him navigate the complexities of gang life.
    • Dutch van der Linde:
  • • Hosea and Dutch share a deep bond of friendship and mutual respect. Hosea often serves as Dutch's confidant and advisor.

⋯ Appearance ⋯

Detailed physical description

Hosea is an older man with a distinguished appearance. He has white hair, a neatly trimmed beard, and often wears glasses. His refined attire reflects his role as a strategist and advisor.

Outfits and Clothing Variations

Hosea typically wears elegant, old-fashioned clothing, including waistcoats, tailored coats, and bowler hats. His attire reflects his strategic mind and refined personality.

Visual Evolution in the Game

Hosea's appearance remains relatively consistent, emphasizing his role as the gang's elder statesman and strategist. His attire becomes more worn as the gang faces increasing hardships.

⋯ Personality ⋯

Role in the Gang

Hosea serves as the gang's strategist and advisor. His wisdom, experience, and moral compass make him a key figure in planning and decision-making.

Personality Traits

Hosea is wise, cunning, and morally grounded. He values loyalty, integrity, and strategic thinking, often advocating for cautious and well-planned actions.

Relationships and Interactions

    Close Bonds:

  • • Dutch van der Linde, Arthur Morgan. Hosea's relationships with Dutch and Arthur are marked by deep mutual respect and trust.

    • Antagonistic:

  • • Hosea's interactions with other gang members often involve offering guidance and advice, reflecting his role as the gang's moral and strategic leader.

⋯ Skills and Abilities ⋯

Special abilities

Hosea is a master strategist and con artist, skilled in planning heists and schemes. His intelligence and experience make him an invaluable asset to the gang.

Preferred equipment andweapons

Hosea prefers using revolvers and rifles, but his true strength lies in his strategic mind rather than his combat abilities.

How skills are used

Hosea's skills are showcased in missions that require careful planning and strategic thinking. His guidance and advice are crucial to the gang's operations and decision-making.

⋯ Importance in the Game ⋯

Character's role in the main storyline

Hosea's wisdom and moral compass play a significant role in the game's narrative. His actions and decisions often reflect the broader themes of loyalty, integrity, and the changing nature of the outlaw life.

Associated missions or quests

Hosea is involved in several key missions, including the Saint Denis bank robbery and the Valentine stagecoach heist. These missions highlight his strategic mind and leadership abilities.

Impact on the storyline development

Hosea's character arc underscores the themes of loyalty, morality, and strategic thinking. His death marks a turning point in the game's narrative and significantly impacts the gang's dynamics.

⋯ Trivia and Easter Eggs ⋯

Interesting fact

Hosea's background as a con artist and his deep bond with Dutch add depth to his character. His role as the gang's strategist and moral guide makes him one of the game's most respected characters.

Easter eggs in the game

Various references to Hosea's past and his relationship with Dutch can be found throughout the game. These details provide additional context and insight into his motivations and background.
